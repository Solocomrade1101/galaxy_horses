Welcome to the Galaxy

Win races, earn BNB. Galaxy Horses is a digital horseracing game, built on the blockchain. Train, breed and race your champion horses against others to earn BNB. Buy additional facilities and build yourself an empire.

Game Features

Racing

Having 10 players compete against each other to win. Use every possible improvement, refine your skills, understand the mechanics and dominate the competition.

Breeding

As with real horses, the bloodline and performance of these NFT horses determine their value, and there is an actual open market in which they can be traded.

Renting

Easy way to start the game without the NFT purchase. Choose a suitable way to rent for the required duration.

Training

Horse and jockey need to keep fit for the races. With regular training, you increase your winning chances.

How can I start playing?

  1. Connect your Wallet to the Binance Smart Chain and Galaxy Horses Website
  2. Click here to view our amazing collectables!
  3. Purchase or rent a single Galaxy Horse from the Liquidifty Marketplace
  4. Enter the race and you'll instantly be in the racetrack that matches your horse's class.
  5. Prepare for the race and start competing against 10 others! If you finish in the top 3 places you win $GHR tokens!
